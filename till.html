Till

Age: 21 Height: 5'10"

Till is a tall, but thin man with fair skin, teal eyes, and shaggy grey hair. On the side of his neck is his name marked in silverish-grey, usually covered in a green patch for most of the series. It isn't until the events of Round 6, where his branding is officially shown.
