Sua

Age: 23 Height: 5'2"

Sua is a young woman of average height with pale skin, violet eyes, and short black hair that reaches mid-way to the nape of her neck. She also has thin strands of hair falling down in front of her face, almost reaching down to her eyelids. Her idol outfit consists of a short white dress with a large bow sitting on top of the hem and off-the-shoulder puffed sleeves that cover her arms completely. She accessorizes with a crescent-shaped headband, pearl earrings, and white ankle boots. It was mentioned that Sua was cute with an angelic beauty or a doll-like beauty. When Sua let her hair grow long for the photoshoot, many boys found her more attractive with long hair than with short hair and fell in love.
