Mizi

Age: 29 Height: 5'4"

Mizi is a young adult with fair skin and long pink hair that gradually transitions into a more cornflower blue hue. Her eyes are golden-hazel or chartreuse in color, and she wears glasses when she is outside of the competition. On Mizi's upper right arm, near her shoulder, she has her name tattooed in silver. It is noted that she has been said as having a pretty face, making her gain several admirers because of that.
